# CardSnap: Business Card Scanner Support

## About CardSnap
CardSnap is a privacy-focused business card scanner that instantly converts business cards into iPhone contacts using on-device processing.

## How to Use CardSnap

### Scanning a Business Card
1. **Open CardSnap** on your iPhone
2. **Point your camera** at the business card
3. **Wait for automatic scanning** - CardSnap will detect and process the card
4. **Review the extracted information** on the results screen
5. **Edit any details** if needed
6. **Tap "Save to Contacts"** to add to your iPhone

### Permissions
CardSnap requires two permissions to function:
- **Camera Access**: To scan business cards
- **Contacts Access**: To save scanned information to your iPhone

## System Requirements
- iOS 16.0 or later
- iPhone with camera
- Contacts app access

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
- Make sure you've granted camera permission to CardSnap
- Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → CardSnap → Enable

### Can't Save Contacts
- Make sure you've granted contacts permission to CardSnap
- Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts → CardSnap → Enable

### Poor Scanning Results
- Ensure good lighting when scanning
- Hold the card flat and steady
- Make sure the entire card is visible in the camera view
- Clean text works better than handwritten text

### App Crashes
- Restart CardSnap
- Restart your iPhone
- Make sure you have the latest version of CardSnap

## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection
CardSnap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or business card data to external servers. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

### Permissions
CardSnap requests the following permissions:
- **Camera Access**: To scan business cards using your device's camera
- **Contacts Access**: To save scanned business card information directly to your iPhone's Contacts app

### Data Processing
- Business card scanning and text recognition is performed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework
- No business card images or extracted data are stored within the CardSnap app
- Contact information is saved directly to your iPhone's native Contacts app under your control
- No data is transmitted to CardSnap developers or third parties

### Third-Party Services
CardSnap does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external services.

### Data Retention
CardSnap does not retain any user data. All scanned information is immediately processed and, if you choose, saved to your device's Contacts app.
